Overview

Purpose

This website's purpose is to make an online presence to our company. This way people will be able to more easily book with us and buy our products to increase our competition.

Audience

The audience of our website will be anyone that is willing and brave enough to do some rafting. Usually our customers are youth or retired. The website will facilitate the way people setup a time for their adventure and be able to buy or rend quality products for their raft. The site is intended to be accessed on all platforms.
